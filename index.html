About IMARPE

Instituto del Mar del Perú (IMARPE) is a specialized technical agency of the Ministry of Production, oriented to scientific research and the study and knowledge of the Peruvian sea and its resources, to advise the state in making decisions regarding the sustainable use of fisheries resources and conservation of the marine environment, contribute to the development of the country.

About the repository

This repository was created to contain principal scientific software used in different research directions. The above packages are:

Joint Jack Mackerel - JJM: https://github.com/imarpe/jjmTools

Pelagic Assessment Tools for Hydroacoustics - PATH: https://github.com/imarpe/path

imarpeTools: https://github.com/imarpe/imarpeTools
